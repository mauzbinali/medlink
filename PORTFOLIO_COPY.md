MedLink - Flutter Doctor Appointment & Telemedicine App

### Portfolio Description

MedLink is a modern Flutter healthcare mobile application designed for Android and iOS. I built it as a complete doctor appointment and telemedicine workflow with separate patient, doctor, and admin portals.

Patients can search doctors by specialty, filter profiles, review ratings, book clinic or online consultations, choose a date and time slot, attach medical reports, select a payment method, manage appointments, chat with clinics, view prescriptions, download receipts, and store medical records.

Doctors can accept or reject booking requests, manage availability, review patient details, start a video consultation UI, write prescriptions, and view earnings. The admin portal includes patient and doctor management, doctor approvals, appointments, payments, revenue metrics, charts, and health tip management.

The app uses Flutter with Riverpod and go_router, includes dark mode, responsive layouts, smooth animations, PDF generation, local storage, notifications, empty/loading states, and a Firebase-ready service layer for Authentication, Firestore, Storage, and Messaging.
